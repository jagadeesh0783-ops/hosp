About the Department

A Legacy of
Orthopaedic Excellence

Founded in 1960 under Dr. P. Rangachari, we have evolved into a premier centre for musculoskeletal care, education, and research.

🏛️

Overview & History

Established in July 1960, the department has evolved into a premier centre for musculoskeletal care. We specialize in arthroscopy, sports medicine, joint replacement, spine surgery, and complex trauma management utilizing contemporary principles.

📚

Academics & Education

A national leader in orthopaedic education offering undergraduate (MBBS) and postgraduate (MS Ortho) training. The department hosts cadaveric courses, workshops, and CMEs in the 100-seat AC Smt. Usha Memorial Hall.

🔬

Research Excellence

An unparalleled rise in orthopaedic research over the last decade. Our clinical and fundamental research projects are backed by national funding, actively advancing treatment protocols for musculoskeletal disorders.

🏥

Patient Care

Thousands of complex orthopaedic surgeries performed annually. Our operation theatres are equipped with advanced, world-class technology ensuring superior patient outcomes through rigorous pre-operative planning and rehabilitation.

Clinical Services

Hospital Services

World-class care available round the clock for every orthopaedic need.

🏥

Out Patient Services (OPD)

Comprehensive daily OPD clinics for general orthopaedics, alongside dedicated speciality clinics for sports injuries, spine disorders, and paediatric deformities.

🕗 Mon–Sat · 8 AM – 12 PM
🛏️

In Patient Services (IPD)

110 dedicated beds spanning distinct male and female wards, with a specialized 21-bed immediate post-operative recovery ward for critical monitoring.

🚨 24 / 7 Trauma & Emergency
